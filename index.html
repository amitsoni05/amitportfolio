About Me

I am a .NET Full Stack Developer with 1.6+ years of professional experience in building and maintaining web applications using ASP.NET Core, C#, Entity Framework, and SQL Server. I’ve worked across multiple domains including healthcare, transport, e-commerce, and HR management, handling both live projects and new development from scratch. I specialize in designing scalable, maintainable applications using layered architecture, Repository and Unit of Work patterns. I also have experience in front-end development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap. I'm passionate about continuous learning, and I’ve deployed several personal and professional projects to enhance my understanding of real-world systems. My goal is to create reliable, user-focused solutions that add real value to both clients and teams.
